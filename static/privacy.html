Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 30, 2024

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Email address and basic profile information
  • Business Information: For businesses posting deals - business name, location, and contact details
  • Location Data: With your permission, to show you relevant local deals in your area
  • Transaction Information: Purchase details when you buy deals through our platform

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Show you local deals and businesses near you
  • Process transactions when you purchase deals
  • Enable businesses to manage their offers
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve our platform and services

3. Information Sharing

We share your information only when necessary:

  • With businesses when you purchase their deals (so they can fulfill your purchase)
  • With payment processors (Stripe) to complete transactions securely
  • When required by law

4. Your Control

  • You can update your account information anytime
  • You can disable location services in your device settings
  • You can delete your account by contacting us
