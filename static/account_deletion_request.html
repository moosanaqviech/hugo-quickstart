Please include in your email:

⚠️ Important Notice

• Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone

• All your business data, deals, and analytics will be permanently removed

• Any active deals will be immediately deactivated

• You will lose access to all app features and historical data

⏱️ Processing Timeline

We will process your deletion request within 30 days and send you an email confirmation once your account and all associated data have been permanently deleted from our systems.
